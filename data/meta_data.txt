About data
----------

1. heart.csv

link to dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/ronitf/heart-disease-uci

age: age in year
sex: male 1, female 0
cp: chest pain type (4 values)
trestbs: resting blood pressure
chols: serum cholestoral in mg/dl
fbs: fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl
restecg: resting electrocardiographic results (values 0,1,2)
thalch: maximum heart rate achieved
exang: exercise induced angina
oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest
slope: the slope of the peak exercise ST segment
ca: number of major vessels (0-3) colored by flourosopy
thal: 3 = normal; 6 = fixed defect; 7 = reversable defect
goal or target: 0 for absnse in any heart disease, 1 for presence of any heart disease.
